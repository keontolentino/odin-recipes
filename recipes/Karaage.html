Karaage Recipe

Description

Karaage is a deep fried chicken marinated in a sweet sauce and coated with potato starch for crispy texture, crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh
  • Potato Starch
  • Canola Oil
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. To create the marinade, grate 2 cloves of garlic, grate 1 tablespoon of ginger, put 1 cup of sake, put 1 cup of mirin, put 1 cup of soy sauce
  2. Season the chicken thigh with salt and pepper
  3. Put the chicken thigh in the marinade and let it sit for atleast one hour, 12 hours maximum
  4. Coat the chicken thigh with the potato starch
  5. In a deep frying pan, put canola oil in, put the stove on medium heat
  6. Deep fry the chicken thigh for 2:30 minutes
  7. Take out the chicken thigh and let it rest for 2:00 minutes
  8. Re-fry the chicken thigh for 30 seconds then take it out and let it rest
