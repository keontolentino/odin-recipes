Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Teriyaki Chicken is a grilled chicken coated with a sweet soy sauce based sauce

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green Onion
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Potato Starch
  • Chicken Thigh
  • Canola Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. In a pan,broil the Ginger, Garlic, Green Onion, and Onion until it is charred
  2. Put in 1 cup of Soy Sauce, 1 cup of Mirin, 1/2 cup of Sake, and 1 cup of Sugar
  3. Bring the pan into a boil and let it simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Season the Chicken Thigh with Salt and Pepper
  5. Coat the Chicken Thigh with the Potato Starch
  6. In a separate pan, fry the Chicken Thigh in Medium Heat using the Canola Oil
  7. Once the chicken is crispy on the outside, lower the heat
  8. Brush the teriyaki sauce made earlier on the chicken until it's fully coated over low heat
