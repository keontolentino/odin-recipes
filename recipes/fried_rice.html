Fried Rice

Home

Description

Fried rice is a versatile dish that can include a variety of meat and vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 shallot
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger
  • 4 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 chopped green onion
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. pour 2 tablespoons of oil in the pan and bring it up to temperature
  2. Cook the egg scrambled then place it aside
  3. pour 2 tablespoons of oil into the pan and bring it up to temperature
  4. cook the garlic until fragrant then place the shallots in
  5. pour the rice into the pan and mix well
  6. pour the soy sauce in the rice and mix well
  7. put half of the green onion into the rice and cook for 20-30 seconds
  8. Plate the rice and put the rest of the green onions on top of the rice as garnish
