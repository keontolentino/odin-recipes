Description

Tonkatsu is a deep fried pork cutlet covered with a crispy bread crumbs

Ingredients:

  • Pork loin
  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Neutral oil of your choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Coat the pork loin with all purpose flour
  2. Coat the pork loin with eggs
  3. Coat the pork loin with bread crumbs
  4. Pour enough oil in a pan for deep frying and bring it up to temperature
  5. Carefully place the pork loin into the pan and fry it for 5 minutes
  6. Take it out of the pan and it's ready to eat!
