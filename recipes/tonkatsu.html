Tonkatsu

Description

Tonkatsu is a deep fried pork cutlet covered in breadings, it is juicy in the inside and crispy on the outside. It also have a tonkatsu sauce on the side that is both sweet and savory.

Ingredients

  • Pork Chops
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 2 Eggs
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Ketchup
  • soy sauce
  • sugar
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Canola Oil

Steps for the pork

  1. Season the pork with salt and pepper
  2. Coat the pork with flour
  3. Coat the pork with eggs
  4. Coat the pork with panko bread crumbs
  5. Put the oil in a deep frying pan for deep frying at medium heat
  6. Put the pork in the deep frying pan for 2:30 minutes to 3:00 minutes
  7. Let it rest for 2:00 minutes

Steps for the dipping sauce

  1. Put 4 tablespoon of ketchup
  2. Put 2 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  3. Put 2 tablespoon of Oyster Sauce
  4. Put 2 tablespoon of soy sauce
  5. Put 2 tablespoon of sugar
  6. Mix all the ingredients well
