Bulgogi

Description

Bulgogi is a marinated thin slices of beef that has a sweet and savory flavor

Ingredients:

  • 5 table spoons of soy sauce
  • 1/4 chopped green onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lbs flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Created the marinade by mixing the soy sauce, green onion, sugar, garlic, sesame seeds/oil, black pepper into a bowl
  2. Place the flank steak into a bowl and pour the marinade in, cover and refrigerate for a few hours or overnight
  3. Pour the vegetable into a pan and bring it up to temperature
  4. Grill the meat until slightly charred and its ready to eat!
